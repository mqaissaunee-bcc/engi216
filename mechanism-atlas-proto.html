ENGI 216 · Dynamics & Kinematics of Machines

Mechanism Atlas

Real machines and the kinematics underneath them. Flip any card between the machine and its skeleton diagram — the mobility, joints, and Grashof class come along for the ride. Each card has its own play button.

Thirteen mechanisms across five motion families. The slider-crank, four-bar, parallelogram, and toggle reuse the circle-intersection / slider-crank solvers from the standalone modules; gears share one tooth-drawing engine. The whole library is the MECH data array — each entry is one config object plus a draw function.

Built in the ENGI 216 blueprint style · WCAG 2.1 AA targets · single self-contained file · all diagrams original.
